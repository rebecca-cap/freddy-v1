1Status as chips, not a dropdown. It's the control traders touch constantly (the kickoff "80% case"). One click, no menu. "All" is the default selected chip.
2Product & Date are dropdowns. More options, set less often — they don't earn permanent horizontal space as chips.
3Count + Clear all live on the right, always visible. With no filters, the count shows the full set and Clear all is disabled — establishing the anchor before anything is filtered.
