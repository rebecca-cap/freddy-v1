# Round 1 brief

Resolve the **layout and interaction model** for the QuoteBook filter bar.

## Focus

- Where the bar sits (between page header and table) and what's on it.
- Status as one-click **chips** vs Product/Date as **dropdowns** — does the split feel right?
- **Additive (AND)** filtering, demonstrated with "Pending + Gasoline + Today".
- The always-visible **result count + Clear all** anchor (the "never look empty" requirement).
- A single **sort** control on the bar (date / product / status).

## Out of scope (this round)

- Zero-results / empty state treatment — Round 2.
- Sticky-on-scroll behavior — Round 2.
- Active filters as removable tokens — Round 2 question.
- Saved views, per-column grid filters, multi-sort — out of project scope.
