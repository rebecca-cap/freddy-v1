Motion & micro-interactions

Motion is present but restrained — it signals a calm "thinking machine," never bounces or distracts. Every effect is slow, low-contrast, and purposeful; together they make the empty state feel alive, the work-in-progress feel legible, and view changes feel spatially grounded.

The signature effect is the shimmer border that rings the panel: a multi-color ring sweeping indigo into violet into magenta, with two glints orbiting the edge at different rates and directions so the panel reads as actively reasoning. At rest it stays a crisp 2px ring with no heavy glow. The same restraint governs the thinking state inside the body: live thinking steps and the inter-message thinking… divider play a gentle gradient sweep only while a turn is active, settling to static once the answer resolves.

Spatial transitions reinforce the panel's information architecture. The panel enters with a short upward translate plus fade. Switching between views animates as a horizontal push/pop whose direction matches the nav direction, so moving deeper and backing out feel like physical movement. Two small ambient touches add warmth: the welcome mascot drifts in a slow idle sway, and suggestion chips fire a one-shot diagonal gradient glimmer on hover.
