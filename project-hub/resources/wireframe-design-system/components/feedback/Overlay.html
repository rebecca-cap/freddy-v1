Overlay Components

Modals and drawers for dialogs, forms, and slide-in panels.

Modal

Centered dialog overlays for focused interactions. Available in default, small, large, and full sizes.

Default Modal (500px)

Modal Title

Modal content goes here. This is a default-sized modal that works well for most use cases.

Small Modal (400px)

Small Modal

A compact modal for simple confirmations.

Modal Variants

Modals can be configured with or without headers and footers.

Modal with Header Only

Information

This modal has no footer. The close button in the header is the only way to dismiss it.

Modal with Body Only

A minimal modal with just content and inline actions.

Drawer - Right

Slide-in panels from the right edge. Available in narrow (400px), default (600px), and wide (800px) widths.

Right Drawer (Default Width)

Drawer Title

Drawer content goes here. This panel slides in from the right edge.

Narrow Drawer (400px)

Filters

Narrow drawer for filters or settings.

Drawer - Bottom

Slide-up panels from the bottom edge, useful for mobile-friendly interactions.

Bottom Drawer

Select Option

Bottom drawer content. Great for action sheets on mobile.

Usage

Copy and paste these patterns into your wireframes.

<!-- Modal (centered) -->
<div class="wf-overlay wf-overlay-visible">
  <div class="wf-overlay-backdrop"></div>
  <div class="wf-modal">
    <div class="wf-modal-header">
      <h2 class="wf-modal-title">Modal Title</h2>
      <button class="wf-modal-close">&times;</button>
    </div>
    <div class="wf-modal-body">
      Modal content goes here
    </div>
    <div class="wf-modal-footer">
      <button class="wf-button wf-button-secondary">Cancel</button>
      <button class="wf-button wf-button-primary">Confirm</button>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- Modal Sizes: wf-modal-sm, wf-modal-lg, wf-modal-xl, wf-modal-full -->

<!-- Drawer (from right) -->
<div class="wf-overlay wf-overlay-visible">
  <div class="wf-overlay-backdrop"></div>
  <div class="wf-drawer wf-drawer-right">
    <div class="wf-drawer-header">
      <h2 class="wf-drawer-title">Drawer Title</h2>
      <button class="wf-drawer-close">&times;</button>
    </div>
    <div class="wf-drawer-body">
      Drawer content
    </div>
    <div class="wf-drawer-footer">
      Actions
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- Drawer Directions: wf-drawer-right, wf-drawer-left, wf-drawer-bottom, wf-drawer-top, wf-drawer-full -->
<!-- Drawer Widths: wf-drawer-narrow (400px), default (600px), wf-drawer-wide (800px) -->
