Alert Components

Inline alert banners for displaying important messages within the page flow.

Alert Variants

Four semantic variants for different types of messages: info, success, warning, and error.

ℹ

Information

This is an informational alert with a title and description. Use it to share helpful context with users.

✓

Success

Your changes have been saved successfully. You can continue editing or return to the dashboard.

⚠

Warning

Your subscription will expire in 7 days. Please update your payment method to avoid service interruption.

✕

Error

Unable to process your request. Please check your input and try again, or contact support if the problem persists.

Simple Alerts (Message Only)

Compact alerts with just a message, useful for brief notifications.

ℹ

You have 3 new notifications.

✓

Email sent successfully.

⚠

Please review the highlighted fields before submitting.

✕

Connection failed. Please check your network.

Alerts with Actions

Alerts can include action buttons to let users respond to the message.

⚠

Unsaved Changes

You have unsaved changes that will be lost if you leave this page.

ℹ

New Feature Available

Check out our new dashboard analytics. See your data in a whole new way.

✕

Payment Failed

We couldn't process your payment. Please update your billing information.

Non-Closable Alerts

Important alerts that cannot be dismissed.

✕

Account Suspended

Your account has been suspended due to a policy violation. Please contact support for assistance.

⚠

This feature is currently in beta. Some functionality may be limited.

Banner Alerts

Full-width banners for page-level notifications, typically placed at the top of the content area.

ℹ

Scheduled maintenance: The system will be unavailable on Sunday, Feb 1st from 2:00 AM - 4:00 AM EST.

✓

Welcome! Your account has been created. Complete your profile to get started.

⚠

Your trial expires in 3 days. Upgrade now to keep access to all features.

Usage

Copy and paste these patterns into your wireframes.

<!-- Alert with Title and Description -->
<div class="wf-alert wf-alert-info">
  <span class="wf-alert-icon">&#8505;</span>
  <div class="wf-alert-content">
    <h4 class="wf-alert-title">Information</h4>
    <p class="wf-alert-description">This is an informational message.</p>
  </div>
  <button class="wf-alert-close">&times;</button>
</div>

<!-- Simple Alert (message only) -->
<div class="wf-alert wf-alert-success wf-alert-simple">
  <span class="wf-alert-icon">&#10003;</span>
  <div class="wf-alert-content">
    <p class="wf-alert-description">Changes saved successfully.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- Alert with Actions -->
<div class="wf-alert wf-alert-warning">
  <span class="wf-alert-icon">&#9888;</span>
  <div class="wf-alert-content">
    <h4 class="wf-alert-title">Unsaved Changes</h4>
    <p class="wf-alert-description">You have unsaved changes.</p>
    <div class="wf-alert-actions">
      <button class="wf-button wf-button-secondary wf-button-sm">Discard</button>
      <button class="wf-button wf-button-primary wf-button-sm">Save</button>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- Full-width Banner -->
<div class="wf-alert wf-alert-error wf-alert-banner">
  <span class="wf-alert-icon">&#10005;</span>
  <div class="wf-alert-content">
    <p class="wf-alert-description">System maintenance in progress.</p>
  </div>
  <button class="wf-alert-close">&times;</button>
</div>

<!-- Variant Classes -->
<!-- wf-alert-info, wf-alert-success, wf-alert-warning, wf-alert-error -->

<!-- Modifier Classes -->
<!-- wf-alert-simple: For message-only alerts -->
<!-- wf-alert-banner: Full-width banner style -->
