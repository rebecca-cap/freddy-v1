Outlined vs Elevated

Cards can have a border (outlined) or shadow (elevated).

Outlined Card

Default card style with a subtle border. Works well on white or light backgrounds.

Elevated Card

Card with a shadow instead of a border. Creates visual depth and hierarchy.

Flat Card

No border or shadow. Useful for nested cards or when grouping is implied by layout.
